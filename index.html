Mastering Market Dynamics for Profitable Hosting

Story

Welcome to an exploration of the Airbnb landscape across three cities in Belgium: Antwerp, Ghent, and Brussels, with a focus on their interactions with the housing market.
Put yourself in the shoes of an investor or a potential Airbnb host, explore and learn!

Click on these key factors to discover their influence in our story

Location and it's influence on prices

The price of an Airbnb is significantly influenced by its location. Various factors come into play when determining the cost based on where the property is situated.
In Antwerp and Ghent we notice that prices are significantly higher in the city center. In Brussels we see that there is a clear divide between the rich south municipalities and the cheaper northern municipalities.

Conclusion

The best property to invest in to rent on Airbnb is close to the city center, but in a cheaper neighbourhood.

The importance of labels

When assigning labels to your Airbnb, think twice, because this could significantly influence your revenue.
When looking at a loft and a condo, there is a big difference in price, but the properties don't differ that much.

"Labels are an important part of hosting an Airbnb"

More rooms, higher price, but is it worth the investment?

Exploring the correlation between the number of rooms in an Airbnb and its price reveals an interesting dynamic.

"The higher the number of rooms, the higher the price"

"We notice the same trend in the housing market, but the return on investment is lower"

Conclusion

The best investment for an Airbnb rental is a one bedroom property

The better the reviews, the more you can ask!

"Notice how the price of the airbnbs goes up, when the review scores are higher"

"Being a superhost correlates with having higher reviews, so treat your guests!"
