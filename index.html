Mastering Market Dynamics for Profitable Hosting

Story

Welcome to an exploration of the Airbnb landscape across three cities in Belgium: Antwerp, Ghent, and Brussels, with a focus on their interactions with the housing market.
My project is designed to assist investors and people seeking properties to list on Airbnb.

Put yourself in the shoes of an investor or a potential Airbnb host, explore and learn!
